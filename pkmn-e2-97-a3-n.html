PKMN ◣ n (also known as just n) is a Fighting-type glitch Pokémon only found in Pokémon Red and Blue. It is owned by many Glitch Trainers. Its cry is the melody of the move Sing. When one views its summary screen, a slower version of the Mt. Moon theme is played.

When defeated in battle, the 'n' of its name remains at the top of the screen, and gradually fills it before the battle continues or ends. It learns many glitch moves and moves of other types, including TM35 (twice), TM05, and TM50.

It occupies Hex CC (204). If it is traded to the Yellow version, it becomes a .8.
