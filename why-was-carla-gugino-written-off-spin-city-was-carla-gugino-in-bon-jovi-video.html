Carla Gugino is an American actress. After appearing in Troop Beverly Hills and This Boy’s Life, she received recognition for her starring roles as Ingrid Cortez in the Spy Kids trilogy, Rebecca Hutman in Night at the Museum, Laurie Roberts in American Gangster, Det.

Gugino was born in Sarasota, Florida, to Carl Gugino, an orthodontist of Italian descent, and a mother of English-Irish descent described as “Bohemian.”

Her parents separated when she was two, after which she traveled between her father and half-brother Carl Jr.’s home in Sarasota, and her Paradise, California, home, to which her mother moved her when she was four.

In mid-2012, Gugino had a lead role as Susan Berg, a Washington, D.C., investigative reporter, on the USA Network’s miniseries Political Animals.

In 2015, she had a lead role in the disaster film San Andreas, in which she once again starred opposite Dwayne Johnson.
