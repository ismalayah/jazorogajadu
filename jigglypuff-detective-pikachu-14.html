For more information on this Pokémon's species, see Jigglypuff.

Jigglypuff (Japanese: プリン Purin) is a Fairy-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the Detective Pikachu subset.

Card text

Healing Melody
いやしのうた
Heal 10 damage from each of your Pokémon.

Trivia

Origin

Healing Melody's Japanese name is that of Sweet Song, a Warrior Skill in Pokémon Conquest. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Ultra Sun.
