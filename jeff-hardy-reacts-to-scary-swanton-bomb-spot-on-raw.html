Jeff Hardy Reacts To Scary Swanton Bomb Spot On WWE RAW

Publish date: 2024-05-19

Jeff Hardy managed to finally put an end to his rivalry with Elias last night after beating The Drifter in a somewhat bizarre Symphony of Destruction match.

The biggest spot of the bout came when Jeff nailed Elias with a devastating Swanton Bomb, but in the process, hit the back of his head on the steel steps.

Jeff took some time to explain what happened in his post-match interview, noting that it “looked a lot worse than it actually felt”.

As we look ahead to the future, many are wondering what the next step could be for Hardy as he continues to build up some momentum on RAW.
