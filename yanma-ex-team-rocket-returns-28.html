Yanma (Japanese: ヤンヤンマ Yanyanma) is a Grass-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the EX Team Rocket Returns expansion.

Card text

Quick Charge
クイックチャージ
Search your deck for up to 4 different types of basic Energy cards, show them to your opponent, and put them into your hand. Shuffle your deck afterward.
Swift
スピードスター		20
This attack's damage isn't affected by Weakness, Resistance, Poké-Powers, Poké-Bodies, or any other effects on the Defending Pokémon.

Trivia

Origin

Swift is a move in the Pokémon games that Yanma can learn.
